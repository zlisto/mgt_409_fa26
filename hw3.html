Homework 3: Customer Catalog Co-Pilot

Covers Lectures 5–6  |  Core MBA lens: Customer

Build a shopper-facing website with multimodal RAG—think fashion retailer, furniture, real estate listings, or any catalog business you know.

Requirements

  1. Text RAG: Ingest policy or FAQ documents (returns, shipping, sizing). Answers must cite sources and refuse when not in the KB.
  2. Image catalog RAG: Ingest ≥20 product images with metadata (name, price, category).
  3. Two query types: text question → cited answer; image upload → similar in-catalog products.
  4. Mini eval: 5 test queries (3 text, 2 visual)—what worked and what failed.

Submission

Submit hw3.zip with code, requirements.txt, sample catalog files, and your evaluation write-up.
