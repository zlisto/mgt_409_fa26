Homework 2: Competitor Intelligence Agent

Covers Lectures 3–4  |  Core MBA lens: Competitor

Competitive strategy homework—except your analyst is an agent. Two parts: visual analysis plus web research.

Part 1 — Competitor image / ad analysis

  1. Upload a competitor product photo or advertisement.
  2. Extract structured claims and attributes (product type, price if visible, key messages, target audience).

Part 2 — Competitive landscape research

  1. Given a niche you choose, build a web-search agent that produces a competitor table.
  2. Each row must include source URLs for factual claims.
  3. Include a short note on one hallucination or unverified claim you caught.

Submission

Submit hw2.zip with code, requirements.txt, sample JSON from Part 1, and research export (CSV or Markdown) from Part 2.
